    • About Dwayne Johnson

    Dwayne Johnson, also known as "The Rock," was born on May 2, 1972, in Hayward, California. He is a former professional wrestler and football player. After retiring from wrestling, he became a successful actor, starring in numerous blockbuster films such as the "Fast and Furious" franchise, "Jumanji," and "Moana." Johnson is also a successful entrepreneur, owning his own production company and tequila brand. He is widely regarded as one of the most influential and highest-paid actors in Hollywood.
